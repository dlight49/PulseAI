# Pulse AI: The Impossible Roadmap 🚀
**Objective:** Build a revenue-generating autonomous agent ecosystem ("Pulse AI") to fund PrimeStyle's AR vision.

---

## 🧠 Phase 1: The Master Brain (Foundation)
*   **LLM Integration:** Connect to Gemini 1.5 Pro and GPT-4o for deep reasoning.
*   **Contextual Memory:** A local database that remembers customer preferences, past orders, and friend vs. client status.
*   **The "Persona" Engine:** A system where each business can "train" their agent by uploading their price list, brand voice, and FAQs.

## 📱 Phase 2: The Inbound Sales Rep (WhatsApp & Instagram)
*   **Official WhatsApp Integration:** Using the Meta Cloud API for stable, professional communication.
*   **Instagram Auto-DM:** The agent responds to "How much?" or "Where are you located?" instantly.
*   **Appointment Booking:** Integration with Google Calendar or Calendly to book meetings without human intervention.
*   **Payment Gateway:** Integration with **Paystack** or **Flutterwave** to generate payment links inside the chat.

## 🏹 Phase 3: The Hunter (Autonomous Lead Gen)
*   **Web Researcher:** The agent uses Search APIs (Tavily/Google) to find new prospects based on a niche (e.g., "New business owners in Lagos").
*   **Deep Study Engine:** The AI "visits" the prospect's website and social media to understand their business deeply.
*   **Personalized Proposals:** Using GPT/Gemini to write a unique, 3-paragraph pitch for every single prospect found. No templates.
*   **Automated Outreach:** Sending the pitch via Email, LinkedIn, or Instagram DM using secure Access Tokens.

## 🛠 Phase 4: The "Impossible" Features (Suggested)
*   **Voice-to-Voice (WhatsApp):** The agent can receive and send **Voice Notes** in a human-sounding voice (using ElevenLabs or OpenAI TTS). Perfect for the Nigerian market.
*   **Multilingual/Pidgin Support:** Training the agent to speak perfect Nigerian Pidgin and local languages to build trust with customers.
*   **AR Showcase (PrimeStyle Synergy):** The agent can send a link that opens a customer's camera to "Try On" products via the PrimeStyle AR engine directly from WhatsApp.
*   **Autonomous Social Posting:** The agent creates its own graphics (using DALL-E 3/Midjourney) and posts them to Instagram/X based on trending topics in Nigeria.

## 💰 Phase 5: Scaling & Monetization
*   **Client Dashboard:** A simple web app where business owners can see how many leads Pulse AI found and how many sales it closed.
*   **Subscription Engine:** Automated monthly billing for your clients.
*   **Agency Model:** Hire "Agent Managers" to oversee 100+ bots for 100+ businesses.
